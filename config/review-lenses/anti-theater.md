# Anti-Theater (no fixture-greens)

## Checklist

- [ ] no-fixture-greens: Fixtures do not pre-bake the asserted outcome.
- [ ] no-mocking-subject: The unit under test is not mocked.
- [ ] assertions-bite: Weakening the implementation would flip at least one assertion.

## Verdict contract

Address every checklist item explicitly as pass / finding / NA in your review output.
