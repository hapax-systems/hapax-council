This started because I wanted to plug a MIDI instrument into my PC.

## What This Is

Hapax is a single-operator system that combines:
- **200+ AI agents** coordinated through a stigmergic cognitive mesh
- **150+ pieces of studio equipment** in a queryable perceptual registry
- **Formal governance constitution** with 5 axioms mechanically enforced at every commit
- **GPU visual expression surfaces** (compositor, shader vocabulary, imagination daemon)
- **Voice daemon** with STT/TTS and autonomous speech capability
- **24/7 livestream** as research instrument and evidence surface

Built by a neurodivergent operator (ADHD + autism) as externalized executive function infrastructure. The system compensates for task initiation, sustained attention, and routine maintenance challenges by design, not as accommodation.

## Research

The system is simultaneously a research instrument for studying:
- Human-AI collaboration in sustained, real-stakes environments
- Stigmergic coordination among heterogeneous autonomous agents
- Constitutional governance applied at deployment time, not training time
- Non-anthropomorphic personage in AI systems
- Substrate-independence of cognitive architecture

## Governance

Five axioms, weighted and mechanically enforced:

1. **Single user** (100) - one operator, always
2. **Executive function** (95) - compensate for ADHD/autism, never add cognitive load
3. **Corporate boundary** (90) - work data in employer systems only
4. **Interpersonal transparency** (88) - no persistent state on non-operator persons without consent
5. **Management governance** (85) - LLMs prepare, humans deliver
