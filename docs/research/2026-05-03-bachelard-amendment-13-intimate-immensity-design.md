# Bachelard Amendment 13 — Intimate Immensity (the Seventh Axis)

## Decision

**Amendment 13 is the Phenomenology of Intimate Immensity** (*La poétique de l'espace*, ch. VIII). A12 closed at "6 axes is the LIMIT of canonical register" — A13 must JUSTIFY a 7th distinct axis.

Justification: Intimate Immensity is the discovery that **immensity can be FOUND WITHIN the small, the cellar, the held interior** — the cosmos found in the bedroom, infinity found in a single moment of reverie. *"Immensity is within ourselves. It is attached to a sort of expansion of being that life curbs and caution arrests, but which starts again when we are alone."* (Bachelard ch. VIII §1).

Distinct from existing axes:
- **A5 Outward Immensity** — immensity AGAINST the outside (sky, horizon)
- **A8 Miniature** — scale-detail / inward-detail
- **A11 Vertical (cellar)** — the dwelling's depth-pole
- **A13 Intimate Immensity** — the SUSPENSION of the inside/outside distinction; immensity found WITHOUT going outside

A13's novel axis: **the dialectic-collapse**. Where A12 makes the boundary CHARGED (two-way tension), A13 dissolves the boundary entirely — outside is no longer "the not-inside"; the inside opens onto its own immensity. This is genuinely orthogonal to all 6 existing axes because it operates on the *meaningfulness* of the inside/outside distinction itself rather than on the spatial/scale/temporal/perimeter/vertical/boundary dimensions.

## 1. Concept summary

Bachelard's intimate immensity is not the discovery of literal infinity. It is the phenomenology of **immensity-found-within** — *"He who hears with his eyes the bird that lives in the depths of the heart's solitude opens up to immensity within himself"* (ch. VIII §3, after Rilke).

For Hapax Reverie, intimate immensity is the visual register of **interior-as-cosmos**: when the operator is held (A10), focused (A8), settled (A7), and a moment of reverie opens, the field briefly behaves as though it has unbounded depth — not by expanding outward (A5) but by deepening WITHIN. The held interior gains a "gateway-to-infinity" character without losing its protective enclosure.

| Existing axis | What it does | A13 contrast |
|---------------|--------------|--------------|
| A5 Outward Immensity | Field expands OUTWARD (sky-character) | A13 deepens INWARD (cosmos-found-within) |
| A8 Miniature | Detail amplification at scale | A13 immensity at scale (inverse) |
| A10 Nests-Shells | Sheltered enclosure | A13 enclosure becomes gateway |
| A12 Outside-Inside | Charged boundary (two-way) | A13 boundary dissolves into infinity |

A13 is the FIRST amendment to be **genuinely paradoxical**: held + open, sheltered + infinite, intimate + immense. The dialectic is what makes it phenomenologically distinct.

## 2. Relation to Amendments 1-12

A13 is **mostly orthogonal** to all prior amendments — it composes with them rather than competing. Specific interactions:

- **#1 Materialization** — content crystallizes from procedural noise. A13 amplifies the noise GRADIENT inside the nest interior so the substrate reads as having "depth-of-content" rather than uniform texture.
- **#2 Dwelling/Trace** — A13 extends trace persistence WITHIN the held interior (the nest "remembers" with longer lookback).
- **#3 Material Quality** — material_id. A13 pairs naturally with **water** (refractive, internally complex) and **air** (lightness-with-depth). Resists **earth** (substantial, opposite of immensity-feel) and **fire** (dispersive). **Void** is the strongest pairing — voidness within the held interior reads as the pure intimate-immensity character.
- **#4 Reverberation** — feedback echo. A13 deepens the feedback decay length (afterimages persist longer with softer falloff) — reverberation as a marker of interior depth.
- **#5 Outward Immensity** — explicitly the COMPLEMENT. A5 expands outward; A13 deepens inward. Both can fire together but they're aimed at opposite poles. The **A5 + A13 simultaneous** stance is the Bachelardian "house and universe" register: the dwelling is sheltered AND opens onto cosmos.
- **#6 Soft Escalation** — pacing. A13 events are SLOW arcs (3-5s soft escalation; this is reverie-time, not action-time).
- **#7 Roundness** — inward-self / centered. STRONG PAIRING. Roundness's centeredness is the precondition for intimate immensity (you must be centered to discover the cosmos within).
- **#8 Miniature** — inward-detail. STRONG PAIRING. Detail-amplification + immensity-within = "the universe in a grain of sand" (Blake-Bachelard).
- **#9 Drawers** — disclosure. Compatible. A drawer opening WITHIN intimate-immensity is "revelation of the cosmos within" — the most charged disclosure register.
- **#10 Nests-Shells** — perimeter. STRONG DEPENDENCY. The nest's interior is WHERE intimate immensity opens. Without A10's held interior, there's no "inside" to find immensity in.
- **#11 Vertical House** — vertical phenomenology. Compatible. A13 in the cellar register reads as "abyssal-cellar"; in the garret register reads as "infinite-sky-in-the-attic"; both are valid intimate-immensity stances.
- **#12 Outside-Inside Dialectics** — boundary tension. Composable but DIFFERENT register. A12 charges the boundary; A13 dissolves the boundary. They can fire together — boundary is charged AND dissolves into the cosmos within. A12 shows the seam; A13 shows what's beyond it.

A13 has soft dependencies on A7 (roundness centers) and A10 (nest holds) — not hard requirements but the visual register reads strongest with both active.

## 3. 9-dim parameter envelope

A13 is **steady-state** (like A7+A8+A10+A11) but with PARADOXICAL modulation — some dims are AMPLIFIED in the immense direction, others are DAMPENED in the intimate direction:

| Dim | A13 multiplier | Why paradoxical |
|-----|----------------|-----------------|
| **intensity** | × 0.95 (gentle damp) | Reverie-state is calm but PRESENT. |
| **tension** | × 0.50 (strong damp) | Intimacy = relaxation; immensity should not be tense (it's wonder, not anxiety). |
| **depth** | × 1.40 (amplify) | The signature dim — intimate immensity DEEPENS the field. |
| **coherence** | × 1.10 (mild amplify) | Cosmos-found-within is locally coherent (the interior holds shape) but expansive. |
| **spectral_color** | × 0.85 (mild damp) | Restraint within the interior — intimate immensity reads as gentle wonder, not chromatic loudness. |
| **temporal_distortion** | × 0.60 (damp) | Reverie-time is slow time. |
| **degradation** | × 0.90 (mild damp) | Substrate clarity for the gateway character. |
| **pitch_displacement** | × 0.95 | Subtle — the cosmos doesn't oscillate. |
| **diffusion** | × 1.30 (amplify — paradox knob) | The signature paradox: diffusion AMPLIFIED inside the held interior — the field becomes softly cosmic without losing its perimeter. |

The two signature dimensions are **depth × 1.40** (deepens the field) and **diffusion × 1.30** (softens internally without expanding outward). Together they produce the "cosmos within the held interior" character without invoking A5's outward expansion.

A13 is sourced as a derived dimension from `(A7_active ∧ A10_active ∧ low_audio_arousal ∧ stance == "settled" ∧ silence_duration > 60s)`. The strict gating reflects A13's character as a RARE moment — most ticks won't have intimate-immensity active. When it does fire, it fires for an extended period (~30-90s) before fading.

## 4. Shader topology

A13 is implemented as a **steady-state visual_chain modulator + spatial-gradient amplifier**:

1. **Per-tick modulation** at the visual_chain layer (per §3 table) — same pattern as A7+A8+A10+A11.
2. **Spatial-gradient amplifier in `content_layer.wgsl`** — uses A10's `nest_mask()` output to compute an interior-gradient that AMPLIFIES content opacity from edge-to-center within the nest:

```wgsl
// Pseudo-WGSL — applied AFTER A10's nest_mask + A12's boundary_charge.
// Within the nest interior (nest_mask > 0.5), opacity is amplified
// by a gradient that reaches its peak at the centroid. Outside the
// nest, A13 contributes nothing.
fn intimate_immensity_amplifier(uv: vec2<f32>, nest_mask: f32, factor: f32) -> f32 {
    if (nest_mask < 0.5) {
        return 1.0;  // outside or boundary; no amplification
    }
    let centered = uv - vec2<f32>(0.5, 0.5);
    let dist_from_centroid = sqrt(dot(centered, centered));
    // Inverse-distance gradient: peaks at centroid, falls to 1.0 at nest edge.
    let centroid_proximity = smoothstep(0.5, 0.0, dist_from_centroid);
    return mix(1.0, 1.0 + 0.8 * centroid_proximity, factor);
}
```

This produces the "deepening WITHIN the held interior" character — the centroid becomes a gateway-point that the eye reads as opening onto cosmos. NOT a literal lens or vignette — purely an opacity-amplification gradient that reads as depth.

NEW uniform: `u_intimate_immensity_factor` (0..1). Default 0.0 = no amplification; preserves backward compat. Cap at 1.0.

Affected shaders: content_layer.wgsl (primary; the immensity-amplifier) + drift.wgsl (depth-dim modulation per §3) + feedback.wgsl (decay length extension).

The visual_chain layer adds `_apply_intimate_immensity_factor(uniforms, factor)` — sets `u_intimate_immensity_factor` AND applies per-dim modulators per §3.

Composition order in `_uniforms.write_uniforms`:
1. plan_defaults + chain_deltas
2. mode tint
3. roundness bias (A7)
4. miniature bias (A8)
5. nest-shell bias (A10)
6. outside-inside dialectics (A12)
7. **intimate immensity (A13) ← new**
8. vertical-house factor (A11)
9. homage damping
10. drawer event pulse (A9, transient)
11. programme override

## 5. Compositor interaction

### Sierpinski overlays

Sierpinski's fractal subdivision pairs naturally with A13: the recursive self-similarity IS a visual analog of intimate-immensity (each subdivision contains the whole). When A13 is active, Sierpinski subdivision depth COULD be modulated to amplify the recursive-cosmos feel — but this is a soft enhancement, not a hard requirement. Out-of-scope for first ship; namespace it for downstream.

### Token pole

Unaffected by A13. Token pole reports system state independently of phenomenological register.

### Reverie content placement

A13 amplifies content opacity at the centroid (per §4). This composes with A7 (centers content) and A8 (detail-amplifies content) for the "all three" canonical stance: centered + detail-rich + immense-within. The operator's eye reads the centroid as a gateway-point with cosmos-feel.

## 6. Anti-pattern list

The implementation MUST NOT use any of the following — they defeat the intimate-immensity phenomenology:

- **Literal vignette / depth-of-field blur.** A13 is felt depth, not optical depth. A blur reads as lens-trick (defeats Amendment 3 material quality + Anti-Pattern §6 in A12).
- **Particle systems / starfield overlays.** Cosmos-as-stars is a literalism. A13 is the FELT cosmos, not a depicted one.
- **Hard radial gradient that goes full-bright at centroid.** The amplification cap is `× 1.0 + 0.8 * factor` — beyond this, the centroid reads as a hot-spot rather than a depth-gateway.
- **Audio coupling (cosmic-sounding sound effects).** Visual-only.
- **Activation when A10 is OFF.** A13 requires the held interior. The implementation MUST gate `intimate_immensity_amplifier` on A10's `u_nest_factor > 0`.
- **Fast-onset (< 2s) activation.** A13 is reverie-time. The activation arc must follow Amendment 6 soft-escalation with a longer-than-default duration (3-5s typical).
- **A "intimate-immensity" preset.** Modulation regime, not preset.
- **A "intimate-immensity" affordance.** Composition, not selection.
- **u_intimate_immensity_factor > 1.0.** Cap at 1.0.

## 7. Coordination with A7-A12 — seventh orthogonal axis

A13 is the **SEVENTH** orthogonal axis. The complete set:

| Axis | Amendment | Operates on |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Spatial-inward (gather) | A7 Roundness | Drift / breath / hue-rotate-sign-flip |
| Scale-detail (amplify) | A8 Miniature | Noise frequency / contrast / spectral color |
| Perimeter (hold) | A10 Nests-Shells | Spatial nest_mask + per-dim damping |
| Temporal-revelation (event) | A9 Drawers | Transient pulse on disclosure |
| Vertical polarity (dwell) | A11 Vertical House | Per-fragment UV.y modulation |
| Boundary-tension (dialectic) | A12 Outside-Inside | Boundary-zone, requires A10 |
| **Cosmos-within (paradox)** | **A13 Intimate Immensity** | **Centroid-amplification, soft-dependency on A7+A10** |

A13 has soft dependencies on A7 (roundness centers) + A10 (nest holds). Without those, A13 still works mathematically but the phenomenology is weaker.

**The "Maximum Reverie" stance** (all 7 axes ON):
- A7 centers the field
- A8 amplifies detail
- A10 holds the perimeter
- A11 polarizes vertically (cellar/garret/hut)
- A12 charges the boundary
- A13 deepens the held interior into cosmos
- (A9 if a disclosure happens during reverie)

This is the canonical "operator absorbed in deep reverie, present-yet-cosmic, sheltered-yet-infinite" stance. After A13, future amendments need to justify an 8th distinct axis — increasingly hard as the canonical register saturates.

## 9. Validation plan (downstream)

The downstream impl cc-task should pin:

1. `u_intimate_immensity_factor=0` ↔ baseline byte-identical.
2. `u_intimate_immensity_factor=1` AND `u_nest_factor=0` → zero amplification (A13 requires A10's interior).
3. `u_intimate_immensity_factor=1` AND `u_nest_factor=1` → centroid opacity × 1.8 max; nest-edge opacity unchanged.
4. Activation arc follows Amendment 6 soft-escalation with arc_duration ≥ 3s.
5. Strict gating: factor stays at 0 unless ALL of (A7 ∧ A10 ∧ low_arousal ∧ settled ∧ silence>60s).
6. Composition with A7+A8+A9+A10+A11+A12 — all 7 modulators stack; the canonical "Maximum Reverie" stance from §7 produces visibly the deepest visual register.
7. u7 spec-audit pin (#2387) passes after content_layer.json adds intimate_immensity_amplifier_factor.
8. Operator-side acceptance: live livestream — when conditions are met, the held interior should visibly deepen without expanding outward.

## 10. Out-of-scope

- Implementation (`bachelard-amendment-13-intimate-immensity-impl`).
- Audio-domain analogue of intimate immensity (visual-only).
- Sierpinski subdivision-depth modulation when A13 is active (soft enhancement; downstream).
- Operator-tunable amplification cap (default 0.8 fixed; tuning is follow-up).
- A14+ amendments — with A13 the canonical register has 7 orthogonal axes. Further amendments require justifying an 8th distinct axis OR retiring/merging existing ones. The "House and Universe" chapter (II) is the next-most-likely candidate but its phenomenology overlaps with A5 + A13 (house-as-cosmos = intimate immensity at the dwelling scale). DEFER until operator prioritizes.
