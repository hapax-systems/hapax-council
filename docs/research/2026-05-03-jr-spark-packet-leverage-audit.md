Classifications:
- **CONSUMED** — there is a traceable artefact (PR / cc-task note / commit) whose title, body, branch name, or filename references the packet `task_id`.

- **LOST** — no traceable consumption, AND the role/task does not match a known no-op pattern. These are forfeited senior intake.
