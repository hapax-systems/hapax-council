## Public Category

Hapax is a **single-operator autonomous grounding lab**: a live system that
senses a room, reads a vault, recruits agents, governs consent, renders its own
state, and streams the evidence of whether its claims are grounded.

Equivalent short form: **single-operator live epistemic lab**.

Public positioning may say that Hapax studies whether an AI operating
environment can ground itself in one real life, one room, and one public
surface. It must not claim broad-population results, product-market fit,
public safety, rights clearance, monetization readiness, or truth authority
from the dossier alone.

## Private Construction Boundary

This dossier is a private evidence and positioning packet. It can prepare grant
language, application claims, demo framing, artifact notes, support copy, and
licensing context, but it does not authorize publication or monetization.
Public, replay, support, artifact, residency, or licensing use must pass
`conversion-target-readiness-threshold-matrix`, provenance/egress checks, and
public-claim gates before release.

## N=1 Scientific Posture

The defensible method frame is n=1, SCED/BEST-adjacent evidence practice:
bounded single-case observation, repeated measures, explicit intervention
windows, time-aligned artifacts, and claims limited to this operator/studio.

Allowed claims:

- Hapax is an n=1 live epistemic lab for one operator.
- The system can report witnessed evidence about its own runtime state.
- A public artifact may describe a bounded, source-qualified episode when its
  evidence chain is complete.

Forbidden claims:

- Broad-population generalization.
- Medical, clinical, productivity, safety, or universal AI-grounding claims.
- Public/money-facing readiness without target-specific gates.
- Truth, rights, privacy, egress, or monetization authority from aesthetic
  quality, engagement, selection, or operator desire.

## Automation Doctrine

Recurring operator labor is not a business model. If a stream cannot be
automated, legally attested once, or kept as a guarded bootstrap action, it
converts to refusal evidence.

| Class | Meaning | Accepted Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AUTO | System produces and checks the artifact. | Preferred route for publication, replay, receipts, and licensing records. |
| BOOTSTRAP | One-time operator setup that eliminates future manual labor. | Acceptable only when it removes recurring work. |
| LEGAL_ATTEST | Explicit human attestation required by law or grant/application process. | Allowed as a bounded event, not a service loop. |
| GUARDED | Human action allowed behind a written gate. | Must have a refusal path when the gate fails. |
| REFUSAL_ARTIFACT | Blocked opportunity becomes documentation or learning evidence. | Default for hidden support, moderation, consulting, or perk obligations. |

## Anti-Overclaim Rules

Engagement, revenue potential, trend fit, operator desire, aesthetic quality,
selection, scheduling, or command issuance cannot upgrade truth, rights,
privacy, provenance, egress, public status, or monetization status. Refusals
and corrections may prove governance behavior; they do not validate the refused
claim.
