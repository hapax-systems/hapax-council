Weblog: [hapax.weblog.lol](https://hapax.weblog.lol). --- ## what this is Hapax is a single-operator AI research lab building auditable personal AI systems. A coordinated fleet of AI coding agents is governed by five constitutional axioms, 42 tracked council shell hooks, and five portable `agentgov` checks. The tracked council paths use mechanical gates for branch creation, file writes, commits, pushes, pull requests, publication, and selected runtime actions. This is a governed-path claim, not a universal claim that no bypass exists. The system includes attribution and privacy checks designed to fail closed on those paths; broader privacy and consent claims remain scope-bound to labeled data paths and current receipts. The lab runs locally and is operated by one person. Public live egress is readiness-gated; when the YouTube/OBS gate is green, the live channel will be linked as current evidence. --- ## the data Receipt-backed launch-window sample: March 12 through May 10, 2026. **3,041** pull requests opened in the sampled window **2,871** pull requests merged in the sampled window **5** revert-titled pull requests **0.16%** revert-title rate over opened pull requests **42** tracked council shell hook scripts **5** portable `agentgov` checks **47** markdown refusal briefs --- ## why trust A major anti-AI concern is distrust. Hapax treats the causes as engineering requirements and publishes evidence where mechanical gates exist. **Claim discipline** — public launch numbers are receipt-gated and corrected when receipts disagree. Unsupported hard metrics are removed rather than defended. **Slop** — HOMAGE aesthetic governance. CP437 typography, mIRC sixteen-colour semantics. Aesthetic standards are treated as enforceable constraints, not preferences. **Dishonesty** — interpersonal transparency axiom (weight 88/100). Governed data paths reject persistent state about non-operator persons without an active consent contract, modeled as an information flow control lattice. **Privacy** — consent-as-IFC. Operator consent modeled using Decentralized Label Models (DLM/LIO formalism from systems security). Within labeled consent-checking paths, violations are structurally detectable rather than only heuristically guessed. --- ## research Targeting CHI 2027. Research contributions under development: 1. A constitutional constraint with a numeric weight. Higher weight wins conflicts. **consent contract**. A scoped, revocable declaration of what the system may persist or expose about a specific person. **HOMAGE**. The aesthetic grammar. BitchX package — CP437 typography, mIRC sixteen-colour semantics, muted punctuation, bright identity. **HARDM**. The anti-anthropomorphization avatar. The system's refusal of face-identity rendered as visible presence. **GEAL**. Grounding Expression Anchoring Layer. The Sierpinski geometry the system inhabits. Not a face. **programme**. A bounded span of intention. Programme types govern what the system does and why. --- ## support This is independent research with no institutional backing.
