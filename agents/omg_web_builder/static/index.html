what this is

Hapax is a single-operator AI research lab building auditable personal AI systems. A coordinated fleet of AI coding agents is governed by five constitutional axioms, 42 tracked council shell hooks, and five portable agentgov checks. The tracked council paths use mechanical gates for branch creation, file writes, commits, pushes, pull requests, publication, and selected runtime actions. This is a governed-path claim, not a universal claim that no bypass exists. The system includes attribution and privacy checks designed to fail closed on those paths; broader privacy and consent claims remain scope-bound to labeled data paths and current receipts.

The lab runs locally and is operated by one person. Public live egress is readiness-gated; when the YouTube/OBS gate is green, the live channel will be linked as current evidence.

why trust

A major anti-AI concern is distrust. Hapax treats the causes as engineering requirements and publishes evidence where mechanical gates exist.

Claim discipline
Public launch numbers are receipt-gated and corrected when receipts disagree. Unsupported hard metrics are removed rather than defended.
Slop
HOMAGE aesthetic governance. CP437 typography, mIRC sixteen-colour semantics. Aesthetic standards are treated as enforceable constraints, not preferences.
Dishonesty
Interpersonal transparency axiom (weight 88/100). Governed data paths reject persistent state about non-operator persons without an active consent contract, modeled as an information flow control lattice.
Privacy
Consent-as-IFC. Operator consent modeled using Decentralized Label Models (DLM/LIO formalism from systems security). Within labeled consent-checking paths, violations are structurally detectable rather than only heuristically guessed.

vocabulary

legomenon (pl. legomena)
A thing said once. The livestream treats each moment as singular.
axiom
A constitutional constraint with a numeric weight. Higher weight wins conflicts.
consent contract
A scoped, revocable declaration of what the system may persist or expose about a specific person.
HOMAGE
The aesthetic grammar. BitchX package, CP437 typography, mIRC sixteen-colour semantics, muted punctuation, bright identity.
HARDM
The anti-anthropomorphization avatar. The system's refusal of face-identity rendered as visible presence.
GEAL
Grounding Expression Anchoring Layer. The Sierpinski geometry the system inhabits. Not a face.
programme
A bounded span of intention. Programme types govern what the system does and why.
